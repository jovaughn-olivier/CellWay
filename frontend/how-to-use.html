How to Use CellWay

CellWay helps travelers find the best route by considering both speed and cellular network coverage.

1. Creating an Account

Sign up or log in to save your route preferences and access previous routes.

2. Selecting a Route

  • Enter your starting point and destination.
  • Choose your preferred route type: Fastest, Best Coverage, or Balanced.
  • Click "Search" to generate the best route.

3. Viewing the Map

The interactive map will display your selected route with relevant details, including estimated time and coverage information.

4. Saving Routes

Logged-in users can save frequently used routes for easy access later.

5. Multi-Carrier Support

CellWay aggregates data from multiple cellular providers, ensuring accurate coverage information.

6. Adjusting Preferences

Modify your route settings anytime to prioritize speed, coverage, or a balance of both.
